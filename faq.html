Calorie Calculator FAQ

What are macronutrients?

Macronutrients are the three main nutrients your body needs in large amounts: protein, carbohydrates, and fats.

  • Protein: Essential for muscle repair and growth.
  • Carbohydrates: The body's primary energy source for daily activities and workouts.
  • Fats: Important for hormone production and nutrient absorption.

Note: Adequate protein intake can help improve body composition, maintain muscle, and increase satiety.

How do we calculate calories and macros?

  • We use established formulas like the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), similar to apps like MyFitnessPal.
  • Your activity level is factored in to estimate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).
  • We adjust your macronutrient ratios based on your training type, using current sports nutrition research.

How accurate are our calculations?

  • Our calculations provide a reliable starting point based on established nutritional science.
  • Individual factors like genetics, body composition, and health conditions can affect your actual needs.
  • Use these numbers as a baseline and adjust as needed. Consider consulting a registered dietitian for personalized advice.
